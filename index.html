About Me

Hi, I’m Augustine Chidozie, a passionate and results-driven Full-Stack Web Developer with a solid foundation in building dynamic, user-friendly websites and applications. With years of experience transitioning from graphic design to web development, I bring a unique blend of creativity and technical expertise to every project I work on.


I specialize in designing and developing scalable, high-performing websites and web applications tailored to meet client needs. My skill set spans both front-end and back-end technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, ReactJS, PHP (v8.0.28), and robust database management with MySQL.


Currently working at Webcapz Technologies, I’ve collaborated on projects ranging from e-commerce platforms to inventory management systems, delivering solutions that not only look great but also drive measurable results. Whether it’s optimizing a website for performance, integrating APIs, or solving complex development challenges, I’m always up for the task.


Beyond coding, I’m passionate about blockchain technology and continuously learning to stay ahead of the curve in an ever-evolving industry. I also enjoy sharing knowledge, which led me to teach ReactJS and help others grow their web development skills.


When I’m not coding, you’ll find me brainstorming innovative solutions for businesses, particularly in logistics and inventory management, where I aim to simplify operations for small businesses.


Let’s collaborate to bring your ideas to life and create digital experiences that make an impact!

TwiceAsGood

Similar to Udemy, it allows instructors to upload and monetize courses while users can purchase educational e-books. The platform combines seamless frontend and backend technologies for an intuitive experience for both learners and content creators.

AtomPay


ATOMPAY is a web app that allows users to pay for services like affordable data, TV subscriptions (DSTV, GOtv), airtime, bulk SMS, betting account funding, and electricity bills. With a digital wallet, users can easily fund their accounts and make secure payments all in one place.

AlutaHouse


AlutaHouse is a real estate website that allows agents and landlords to effortlessly list properties for rent, lease, or sale, including apartments and land. Individuals can easily search for available properties based on their preferred location. To ensure trust and security, all users are verified using their BVN, NIN, and profile picture, minimizing the risk of scams.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Question by clients


  • Who are you, and what do you do?
I’m Augustine Chidozie, a full-stack web and app developer with expertise in creating modern, functional, and scalable digital solutions. I build websites, web applications, and mobile apps tailored to meet business needs.
  • What services do you offer?
I specialize in:
- Website Development (Business, E-commerce, Real Estate, Schools, etc.)
- Mobile App Development (Android & iOS)
- Web Application Development
- Blockchain & Crypto-related Development
- UI/UX Design Implementation
- Website & App Maintenance and Optimization
  • How long will it take to build my website?
This obviously depends on the size and complexity of the website. We start every project with a commitment to a completion date. Delays do occur but, they are usually related to the client’s: i) changing specifications for the project; ii) slowness in preparing the content to populate the website; and, iii) untimely responses to questions and requests for information.
  • What if all I need is a makeover of my current website?
We do that too. We are committed to addressing each client’s specific needs. Whether this is a brand new site or just an upgrade or refreshing of one that has become a little outdated.
  • Will you help me get my website found on Google?
A business website is virtually useless if it cannot be found by people searching for the goods and services provided by that business. We provide basic search engine and local search marketing services as part of our website development contract. More elaborate services will however require a separate arrangement.
  • How do you know that my new website is working as required?
We perform a number of pre-launch tests and other procedures to make sure that your website is performing as promised and meets industry standards.
